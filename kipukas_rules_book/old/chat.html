Beta Preview — Important Notes

  • • No conversation memory — Each message is independent. Previous context is not remembered.
  • • Expect delays — Limited hardware capacity means requests may queue when busy (30s timeout).
  • • Check facts — Local LLMs can hallucinate and produce inaccurate results.
  • • Token limits — Knowledge base + your prompt must fit within ~4K token context window.
  • • Interactions may be logged — Prompts and responses may be saved for debugging/improvement.
