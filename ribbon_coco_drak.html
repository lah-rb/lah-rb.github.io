Ribbon Coco Drak

  • Scarcity: Unparalleled
  • History:

    Coco spirits take the form of man in selfish death, beast in wrongful death, or a most powerful dragon in a sacrificing death. This spirit has clocked itself with ribbons for an attachment to the physical world.

  • Play Style:

    Requires taming.

  • Weak Points:
    • Sub-critical - Ribbons
    • Critical - Energy essence
